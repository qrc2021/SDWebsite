About CSS

About CSS

CSS is a compact automated license plate reader (ALPR) with the goal of creating an affordable ALPR for everyday drivers to place inside their vehicle. The intention and focus are that the device will easily capture license plate information of other vehicles in the event of a hit and run scenario. Additionally, the system will serve as the basis as an affordable ALPR that can be used for a variety of scenarios in the future, such as traffic analysis, garage management, and more.
